Demographic Information for HazloWeb

What is the population on HazloWeb?

There are 7,721 residents in HazloWeb, 52.13% males and 47.87% females. US-born citizens make up 87.68% of the residents.

Employment statistics in HazloWeb

There are 1,140 entrepreneurs in HazloWeb (21.75% of the workforce); 3,243 workers employed in private companies (61.87%); and 605 people working in governmental institutions (11.54%).

Median and average incomes in HazloWeb

The average annual household income in HazloWeb is $164,004, while the median household income is $77,669 per year.

Marital status of HazloWeb

A total of 2,798 people in HazloWeb have never been married (41.34%), while 2,454 of them are wedded (36.26%).

Education Level in HazloWeb

Approximately 14.62% of the population in HazloWeb holds a high school degree (969 residents), while 18.38% have attained a college certificate (1,192) and 42.07% have a bachelor's degree (2,788).

How many homeowners and renters are there in HazloWeb?

There are 6,300 housing units in HazloWeb. The median year these properties were built is 1981. Of the 3,372 occupied housing units in HazloWeb, 56.49% are owner-occupied and 43.51% have renters living in them.
