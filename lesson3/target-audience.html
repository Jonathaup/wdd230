Description of Target Audience

My target audience will be members of the community looking to make web pages, business owners looking for how to make their website and information on how to make it, and new members of the community looking for the same goals.

Scenarios

  • Is the new community gym a member of our chamber and does it have a website?
  • I want to open an ice cream parlor and I need a website.
  • How many companies have a website?
  • I wonder if there is a sports store in the area?.
  • I want information about supermarkets in our area..

Persona #1 - Rose

Rose is a 30-year-old sports store owner. She has had a small business in her hometown, but she is looking to have her own website. She wants to learn more about how to do it and wants advice and information to make her goal of having a website possible.

Persona #2 - Joseph

Joseph is a 35-year-old gym owner. He has been working in his gym for 4 years and plans to start his business website. He needs to connect with customers in a more practical way and would like help and advice on how to make his website to help his business succeed.
